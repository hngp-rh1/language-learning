Welcome

This browser‑based language learning tool combines the best of Pimsleur, FSI, shadowing, SRS, and comprehensible input. Everything runs locally in your browser using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON‑driven content — no backend required.

The platform is designed for fast, audio‑centric study sessions. You can practice reflex drills, substitution patterns, shadowing, and more. Your progress, notes, and custom drills are stored privately in your browser via localStorage.

Whether you're a beginner or advanced learner, the goal is simple: train your listening, speaking, and recall reflexes efficiently.
